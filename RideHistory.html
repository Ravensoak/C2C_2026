2025 â€“ Scottish Borders

Overview

A route from Gretna Green to Berwick-on-Tweed, following parts of the 'Borderers Way'.

  • Day 1 - Gretna to Carlisle: The ride started in the afternoon with a short route from Gretna Green into Carlisle.
  • Day 2 - Carlisle to Hawick: The route followed quiet country lanes and scenic roads crossing the England / Scotland border before reaching Hawick.
  • Day 3 - Hawick to Wooler: Passing through Kelso allowing us to witness the Women's Tour of Britain before skirting the Cheviot Hills and arriving in Wooler.
  • Day 4 - Wooler to Berwick on Tweed: A detour to the spectacular Bamburgh Castle before taking the coastal path into Berwick. Lindesfarne not visited due to tidal timings.

2024 â€“ Scotland

Overview

A circular route starting and ending near Edinburgh, passing through Stirling and Glasgow.

  • Day 1 - Champany to Stirling: The ride started by visiting the 3 bridge viewpoint in Queensferry before crossing the Forth road bridge and riding the shoreline of the Firth Estuary making use of cycle paths through Dunfermline to Stirling.
  • Day 2 - Stirling to Glasgow: The route headed to the shores of Loch Lomond (with the option to cross Loch Lomond via a ferry) before using cycle paths and approaching Glasgow following the River Clyde.
  • Day 3 - Glasgow to Champany: The route back to Champany followed cycle paths allowing a visit to the Falkirk Wheel, the Kelpies and Blackness Castle along the way.

2021 â€“ Conwy to Belper

Overview

A route starting in Conwy following the north coast of Wales, before making its way inland to the Welsh borders, Shropshire and Staffordshire before arriving in Belper.

  • Day 1 - Conwy to Corwen: The ride started started from Conwy travelling North to Llandudno with a scenic ride around The Great Orme, before following the coastal path to Rhyl, and then in land through Denbighshire on to Corwen
  • Day 2 - Corwen to Eccleshall: The route took us through Llangollen and across the Pontcysyllte Aqueduct to Chirk, before passing Ellesmere Lake and onto Eccleshall.
  • Day 3 - Eccleshall to Home: The route took us into Staffordshire and through the Churnet Valley and back home to Belper.
