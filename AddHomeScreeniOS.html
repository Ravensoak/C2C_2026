Install 2026 C2C Rides

Follow the instructions below to install this site as an App on your Apple device.

  1. Open c2c2026.netlify.app in Safari on iPhone or iPad:
  2. Tap the 3 dots button in the bottom right of the screen.
  3. Tap the icon (the square with an ↑ arrow).
  4. Scroll down and choose “Add to Home Screen”.
  5. Done ✅ You now have an icon on your home page for a one-tap opening of the 2026 C2C Rides site.
