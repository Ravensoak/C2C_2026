Install 2026 C2C Rides

Follow one of the options below to install this site to your Android device.

Option 1: Using Chrome (recommended)

  1. Open Google Chrome on your Android device.
  2. Go to https://c2c2026.netlify.app.
  3. Wait a few seconds, until you see a prompt at the bottom of the screen: "Install app" or "Add C2C Rides to Home screen".
  4. Tap Install.
  5. Done ✅ You now have an icon on your home page for a one-tap opening of the 2026 C2C Rides site.

Option 2: Manual install (if no prompt appears)

  1. Go to https://c2c2026.netlify.app.
  2. Tap the three dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner of Chrome
  3. Choose "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  4. Tap "Add" → choose where you want the icon.
  5. Done ✅ You now have an icon on your home page for a one-tap opening of the 2026 C2C Rides site.
